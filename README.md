# Prodigy WorkforceOS

## Enterprise Workforce Management, Governance, Compliance & Human Capital Management Platform

Prodigy WorkforceOS is a next-generation enterprise platform designed to help organizations manage people, processes, governance, compliance, collaboration, payroll, and workforce intelligence from a single unified ecosystem.

Built for enterprises, government institutions, educational organizations, healthcare providers, and large-scale operations, Prodigy WorkforceOS eliminates fragmented systems by bringing workforce management, operational governance, workflow automation, compliance monitoring, and executive intelligence together in one intelligent platform.

---

## Vision

To become the operating system for workforce management, organizational governance, and enterprise operations across Africa and beyond.

---

## Why Prodigy WorkforceOS?

Many organizations rely on multiple disconnected systems to manage their workforce, payroll, compliance requirements, approvals, communication, reporting, and governance obligations.

Prodigy WorkforceOS consolidates these critical business functions into a secure, scalable, and intelligent platform that enables organizations to operate more efficiently, remain compliant, and make data-driven decisions.
